About International English Academy

Based in Cape Town, South Africa.

Since its establishment in 2004, IEA has continuously pursued effective methods for teaching English to non-native speakers. Through these efforts, it has developed a distinctive educational system grounded in its own philosophy. This system emphasizes close monitoring of learning outcomes, immediate feedback, small class sizes of no more than nine students, immersive cultural experiences, and strong instructional management to ensure engaging and dynamic classes.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest quality English language learning experience to students who wish to achieve conversational or academic proficiency in order to live, work or study in an English speaking environment. By maintaining a low student/teacher ratio (9 students or less per class) and a comprehensive curriculum that focuses on life skills development and cultural immersion, we ensure that the IEA experience is uniquely rewarding for our students.

Our Vision

Our aim is to be one of the leading English language schools in Cape Town - South Africa, providing the best possible English education through small class sizes and affordable tuition.
